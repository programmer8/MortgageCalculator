What formula does this calculator utilize?


\[principle * \frac{i(1 + i)^n}{(1 + i)^n - 1}\]

where:

  • principle = the initial loan amount
  • i = annual interest rate / 12
  • n = number of months

Note: when i == 0, the formula utilized is \(\frac{principle}{n}\)
